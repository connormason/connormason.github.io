About Me

Who is Connor Mason?

Born the child of two engineers, I have been hacking, building, and rebuilding anything I could get my hands on from a very early age. Whether it was Legos as a child, computers as a high-school student, or a whole-ceiling, sound-activated grids of LEDs as a university student, I have always had a project going on. Currently attending University of Michigan, I have combined my love of electronic projects with the ability to control them via software by studying Computer Engineering. I'm not sure what the future holds for me, but I can't wait to help in making the world a better place using technology.
